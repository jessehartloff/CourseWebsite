--- title: Final Video next_lesson: Presentation previous_lesson: Sprint 4 due_date: August 19 --- == Objective
  • Create a short video describing your project

The goal of this video is to quickly show off your project to a non-technical audience. Show what your project does through a demonstration of your running software. Do not scroll through your code and explain how it works.

== Create and upload a video (1 Point)

Create and upload a video to YouTube and add the video link to the teams channel details section on Slack.

== Video is descriptive (1 Point)

The video accurately and clearly describes the current state of your project.

== Demo in video (1 Point)

The video shows a working demo of your project and showcases all the relevant functionality.

== Video is concise (1 Point)

The video is less than 5 minutes in length. Shorter videos are encouraged as long as they cover the previous points.
